ORGANISATION INFORMATION

It is research based consulting firm focusing on governance, social, economic and environmental issues at national and sub nation levels.

It provides technical assistance especially in the field of : Governance and policy; Institutional and capacity development; Survey, research and situation analysis; Finance and economic development; Spatial and evidence based planning and innovation; Planning, monitoring, evaluation, learning and reflection; and Technical and theoretical consultancy services; Detail Project Report (DPR) It has office in Satdobato Lalitpur Nepal with abundance space, information technology, full-fledged physical and power back-up facilities.
